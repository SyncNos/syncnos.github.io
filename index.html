Never lose what you read, watch, or chat. 你问过的、看过的、听过的，都不该丢。

SyncNos WebClipper captures AI chats, web articles, and video transcripts in the background, stores them in your browser first, then lets you sync or export on your terms. SyncNos WebClipper 在后台采集 AI 对话、网页长文与视频字幕：先落浏览器本地事实源，再由你决定同步或导出。

A web clipper touches the most private part of your life: what you read, watch, and ask. SyncNos is designed so you can trust it—by verifying it. WebClipper 触碰的是你最私密的内容：读过什么、看过什么、问过什么。SyncNos 的信任建立在“可验证”之上。

  • Local-first by default 默认本地优先
    Your content lands locally first—then you decide where it goes. 内容先落本地，再由你决定去向。
  • Incremental sync 增量同步
    Only new content gets synced—avoid duplicates, avoid missing gaps. 只同步新内容：不重复，也不遗漏。
  • Auditable behavior 行为可审计
    Every line is inspectable. No black box. 每一行都能看见，没有黑箱。
