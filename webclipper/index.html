What it does 它做什么

WebClipper is designed for “capture first, decide later”—a personal timeline of what you asked, read, and watched. WebClipper 为“先采集、后决策”而生：把你问过、读过、看过的沉淀成个人时间线。

  • AI chat capture AI 对话采集
    Auto-capture supported platforms in the background. 后台自动采集主流平台对话。
  • Web article clipping 网页长文剪藏
    Extract title, author, publish date, and readable body. 提取标题、作者、发布日期与正文内容。
  • Video transcripts 视频字幕
    Capture loaded transcripts from YouTube / Bilibili. 采集 YouTube / B站 已加载字幕。
